FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the qualifications of your nurses and caregivers?

All our nursing staff hold accredited nursing degrees and official practice licenses. Our elderly caregivers are carefully selected and trained in physical assistance, psychological comfort, and caring for patients with Alzheimer's and Parkinson's.

Do you offer 24-hour live-in care?

Yes, we provide 24/7 live-in care services, either through a resident caregiver or via shifts (e.g., rotating 12-hour shifts) to guarantee that the patient is never left unsupervised.

How is the price of the service calculated?

Pricing is calculated based on the patient's medical condition, the type of care (medical treatment vs daily companion care), and the duration (quick visit, 12-hour shift, or 24-hour residency). We offer flexible monthly packages.

How can I book a service or contact you?

You can request a booking by filling out the form below, calling us directly, or using the floating WhatsApp button to chat instantly with our service coordinators.
